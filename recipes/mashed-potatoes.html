Mashed Potatoes

Ingredients

Serves 2

  • 5 small potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • garlic
  • sour cream
  • salt
  • pepper
  • Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Dice the potatoes into small cubes.
  2. Place potatoes in a medium pot along with whole cloves of garlic. Measure the garlic with your heart.
  3. Add water to the pot and cover potatoes by about 2 inches. Salt generously.
  4. Bring the pot to a rolling boil and leave uncovered for 10-12 minutes. Stir occasionally.
  5. Drain the pot. Add butter, two good spoonfuls of sour cream, salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning.
  6. Mash the potatoes, incorporating the butter, sour cream, and seasoning. You can use a wooden spoon and mash against the sides of the pot.
  7. Serve.
